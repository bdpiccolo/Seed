The heatmap and associated sample clustering are based on the taxanomic data. Only a subset of taxa are used to generate the heatmap. The taxa are ranked by the sum of their abundance data. The highest ranked X microbes are used in the heatmap, where X is selected by the user.

List of major functions:

Heatplus:annHeatmap - generates annotated heatmap

Citation for package Heatplus:

Alexander Ploner (2012). Heatplus: Heatmaps with row and/or column covariates and colored clusters. R package version 2.8.0.
